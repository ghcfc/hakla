Upcoming Humanitarian Trip to Iran

We at ST. Michael High School are proud to announce a special humanitarian trip to Iran. The purpose of this journey is to extend our hands of compassion and support to the homeless and poor individuals who are in dire need of care and assistance. This initiative reflects our school’s values of kindness, empathy, and global responsibility.

Our students, guided by teachers and staff, will travel by airplanes to Iran, ensuring a safe and comfortable journey. Once there, we will engage in activities such as distributing food, clothing, and essential supplies, as well as spending time with those in need to bring hope and encouragement. This trip is not only about helping others, but also about learning valuable lessons of humanity, teamwork, and service.

We believe this experience will inspire our students to become responsible global citizens who understand the importance of giving back to society. It is a chance to make a real difference in the lives of people who need us the most.
